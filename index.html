It's a reminder that empathy can be the guiding force in creating better experiences, even beyond the realm of a simple coffee break. As a product designer, this resonates deeply with me - a reminder to infuse empathy into every design, reducing friction and enhancing the overall user experience. It's a philosophy that deserves its place not just in a coffee chat but also in the forefront of a designer's portfolio, elevating it above the tangible product case studies.
