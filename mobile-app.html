Introduction

Picture a classroom where language learning isn't confined to textbooks and rote memorization but unfolds as a dynamic, collaborative adventure. ALP, a mobile application, tailored to make language exploration engaging and effective, fostering a joyful journey for students as they build their speaking skills.



Objective

When I started to collaborate with the ALP team, the app was already in development, but the absence of a dedicated UX-UI Designer had left a gap. Recognizing the opportunity to enhance user experience, I dove into the project headfirst. My design objective became crystal clear:

Revamping the User Journey:
Entering the scene, my mission was to revolutionize the user interface and experience. Guided by simplicity, I led the team through a comprehensive redesign of all screens, streamlining navigation, simplifying colors, and reimagining layouts.

Research & Discovery: Navigating the Unknown Terrain

Joining the team at an advanced stage of development presented a unique challenge - there were no documents, and I had no prior insights into the app. The project had been sailing in what seemed like a hyper-agile manner - requirements communicated verbally, timelines set, and the end result, unfortunately, was a less-than-stellar app.

Upon my arrival, the first task was unraveling the app's primary purpose. It was supposed to cater to both teachers and students, with teachers for evaluation and students for learning. However, the specifics were murky.

Navigating through the existing maze of screens, we discovered redundancy and uncharted territories. Prioritizing screens became a puzzle to solve. We delved into questions: What visual elements were necessary? Did we need sounds and animations for our young audience? Additionally, considering it was meant for small children in schools, the choice of fonts and colors became crucial.

Through this exploration, we not only clarified the app's purpose but also streamlined its structure and identified essential design elements, ensuring a more focused and purposeful development path.



Ideation & Design Process: Crafting Clarity from Chaos

With no solid mockups as my starting point, the ideation and design process unfolded as a journey of simplicity and collaboration. Here's how we transformed chaos into clarity:

Brainstorming the Blueprint:
Our first step involved collective brainstorming sessions. We discussed ideas, I presented wireframes, and we identified visual and audio essentials. This laid the groundwork for what the app would eventually become.

From Concept to Canvas:
As the design started taking shape, I collaborated with the multimedia team to weave graphical visuals and sound elements seamlessly into the user experience. The ongoing collaboration ensured a harmonious blend of design and multimedia elements.

Crafting the User Journey:
The user journey emerged as the guiding thread, weaving together wireframes, mockups, and multimedia elements into a cohesive whole. Weekly meetings became our design canvas, where I presented, received evaluations, iterated if needed, and moved closer to the final vision.

Iterative Refinement:
The design process evolved into a smooth rhythm. Each week brought refinement. It was a dance of ideas, evaluations, and iterations, making the design progressively robust.

Developer Handover and Beyond:
Upon design approval, the torch passed to the developers. The iterative cycle continued as we collaborated closely, ensuring a seamless transition from design to development.



Conclusion

As I pen down this case study, the culmination of our efforts is approaching. The app is tentatively scheduled for a pilot release in the first week of February 2024, a testament to the collaborative journey from chaos to a well-crafted digital solution.
